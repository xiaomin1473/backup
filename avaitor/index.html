AVIATION INFRASTURE
EXPLORE THE MARKET WITH ENORMOURS POTENTIAL

About the Conference

The lifting of economic sanctions unlocks great potential in Iran’s aviation industry. There is pent-up demand for aircrafts, spare parts, MRO facilities, airport infrastructures, and technologies. Furthermore, in view of Iran’s geographical advantage and civilizational heritage, the tourism potentials are expected to lead its international air links to a surge.However, challenges rise behind the positive side.The primary one should be sources of capital.After years’ sanction pressing, Iran’s economy suffers great corrosion,some restraints from sanctions still remain. The element seems to make the major banks become hesitating to rush into Iran’s reviving aviation market. Other challenges highlight the uncertainties of Iran’s policies & laws and lack of skilled staff. Clearly, integrated solutions should be executed to access to international requirements and realize synergies in different industries.

Keeping the circumstances in mind, we organize Aviation Infrastructure Iranunder the theme “Cooperation to Re-vamp Iran’s Aviation Industry”, which intends to highlight the growth challenges and opportunities and strives to provide meaningful coverage on current trends in Iran’s aviation industry. Tailor-made topics will radiate a wide spectrum on aviation policy, airport construction, airline expansion, investment, ATC, MRO, spare parts supply, aviation technology, and safety & security, etc.
